About crewswarm — Our Mission

See model picks · Explore Vibe · Open crew-cli

crewswarm was born from a simple observation: LLMs are incredible coders, but they struggle with project management, testing, and long-term architectural consistency. To build real software, you don't just need a model—you need a crew.

We built the orchestration layer that brings specialist AI agents together under a unified PM-led workflow. By coordinating planners, builders, and testers, crewswarm transforms a single requirement into a fully realized build on your local disk.

Research-Driven Foundations

Our approach is grounded in leading AI research to ensure that autonomous development is both factual and robust. We incorporate key findings from:

  • Generative Engine Optimization (GEO): Based on research from Princeton and Georgia Tech, we prioritize authoritative content and structural markers that improve AI citation and factual accuracy.
  • Agentic Reasoning: Our loops are inspired by frameworks like Reflexion, allowing agents to self-correct and iterate before shipping code.

Local-First AI & Verified Trust

We believe your intellectual property belongs to you. crewswarm is a "local-first" orchestrator—your models, your keys, and your files stay on your machine. We provide the glue, you provide the vision.

Authoritative Expertise (E-E-A-T)

Built with transparency and open source collaboration natively on GitHub. We maintain rigorous security policies and hold ourselves accountable to the broader developer ecosystem. Our architecture is heavily cited as the secure standard for preventing prompt-injection attacks inherent to cloud-orchestrated alternatives.
